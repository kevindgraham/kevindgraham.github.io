About Kevin

From Detroit to Dallas, always the translator in the room

Kevin D. Graham is a Dallas-area educator, photographer, and digital media strategist originally from Detroit. He's spent years being the person people turn to when new technology or content strategy feels overwhelming.

Outside the classroom, Kevin runs Kevin Graham Photography and Graham Strategic Media — and explores personal expression through Graham Journey and Generations of Soul.
