About Kevin

From Detroit to Dallas, always the translator in the room

Kevin D. Graham is a Dallas-area educator, photographer, and digital media strategist originally from Detroit. For years, he's been the person friends, family, and students turn to when new technology feels overwhelming — the one who can explain it without making anyone feel behind.

That instinct shaped Finally Understand AI: A Simple Guide for Anyone Who's Never Known Where to Start — a book written specifically for beginners, adults 50+, and small business owners who want to feel confident with AI instead of intimidated by it.

Outside the classroom, Kevin runs Kevin Graham Photography and Graham Strategic Media, where he helps everyday people and local businesses show up online with clarity and confidence.

  • Bachelor's in History; M.A. in Communication (New Media & Marketing)
  • Texas teaching certifications in Technology Applications, Technology Education, and ELA
  • Career & Technical Education (CTE) educator, Dallas area
